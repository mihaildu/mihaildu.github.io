About me
I am a Software Engineer with more than 6 years experience. I did fullstack web development for more than 5 years, mostly working with React.js, Node.js, Express.js, Next.js, GraphQL & PostgreSQL. Before web development I did low level programming (Linux kernel programming, caching devices etc) in C/C++ and a bit of machine learning in Python and MATLAB. I can pick up new technologies really fast and I'm always up for a good challenge! I enjoy open source a lot, my github profile is linked at the bottom of this page. Feel free to download my CV and look over my past projects. You can also contact me at mihail.dunaev91 [at] gmail.com for business inquiries or just to say hi.

Outside work I am into fitness (bodybuilding, running, hiking, martial arts etc). I even wrote an open source book on fitness. I have a curiosity for computational neuroscience, having studied EEG headsets in university, and I also enjoy experimenting with food (molecular gastronomy), travel & MotoGP!
